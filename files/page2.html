    • About the Developer!

    Denzel Hontanosas is the creator behind ENVIRONAMA, a digital gallery. She is a scholar in Philippine Science High School - Southern Mindanao Campus. She is also a photojournalist and an amateur artist based in Davao city.

    Concept

    ENVIRONAMA was created with the idea of SDG number 13 [Climate Action]. This website project aims to be unique, while it addresses current issues at the same time. Nature has been one of my common subjects for photos and it seems appropriate to showcase its beauty despite the drastic changes in today's society.
